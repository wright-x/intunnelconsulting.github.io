FAQ

Common questions about our process

How long are your contracts?

We work on month-to-month contracts with a 90-day minimum commitment. This ensures we have enough time to build and optimize your systems properly.

Do you own our ad accounts?

No, you always maintain full ownership of your ad accounts. We request access to manage campaigns, but you can revoke access at any time.

How do you handle creative production?

We build a custom UGC creator network for your brand, produce high-converting ad creatives, and maintain a library of tested assets. All creative costs are included in your monthly retainer.

What's the typical ramp time?

Most clients see positive ROAS within 30 days, with predictable pipeline established by day 90. Full system optimization typically takes 6 months.

How does speed-to-lead work?

We set up automated systems to respond to leads within 60 seconds via SMS and email. Our appointment setters then qualify leads using BANT criteria before booking calls on your calendar.

Do you provide setters and closers?

Yes, we provide performance-based appointment setters and expert closers when needed. They're commission-based, so you only pay when they deliver results.
